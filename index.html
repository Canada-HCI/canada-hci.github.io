Canada HCI

Canada HCI aims to promote community, collaboration and shared learning across the Canadian HCI community. This slack channel is a place for academic and industrial researchers to communicate, connect, and share knowledge. The channel is open to anyone interested in learning about and contributing to the Canadian HCI community more generally.

To join the channel, you can request an invitation from any existing member of the community (e.g. your supervisor or lab mates), or alternatively, email: canadahcidotca [at] gmail.com. Please review our etiquette before joining.
