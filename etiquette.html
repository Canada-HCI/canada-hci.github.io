Etiquette

While using the Canada HCI Slack server, we would appreciate everyone follow these guidelines to help foster a welcoming an inclusive community.

General Guidelines

  • Be friendly and welcoming
  • Be patient: Remember that people have varying communication styles and that not everyone is using their native language. (Meaning and tone can be lost in translation.)
  • Be thoughtful: Productive communication requires effort. Think about how your words will be interpreted. Remember that sometimes it is best to refrain entirely from commenting.

Slack Profile Guidelines

  • Full name and photo preferred (include instructions on how to upload)
  • Preferred pronouns in the profile description
  • Include university or industry affiliation / contact info
  • Include a brief summary of research interests or keywords (1-2 sentences)?

Posting Guidelines

  • When posting to a channel, take a moment to read the channel overview and think about whether your message is relevant to all members of the channel - if not, there may be a more appropriate channel
  • When responding to a post, take a moment to think about whether all members of the channel will be interested in your discussion, or if your response would be better suited as a DM to the original poster
